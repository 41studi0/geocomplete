Example: Bounds Biasing

Back to Documentation

Try searching for "Pizza". Results should be biased within Sydney.
For further details on result biasing: http://code.google.com/apis/maps/documentation/javascript/places.html#adding_autocomplete

Source:

var bounds = new google.maps.LatLngBounds(
  new google.maps.LatLng(-33.8902, 151.1759),
  new google.maps.LatLng(-33.8474, 151.2631)
);

$("#geocomplete").geocomplete({
  map: ".map_canvas",
  types: ['establishment'],
  bounds: bounds
});
